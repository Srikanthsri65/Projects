Capstone Project : Analysis of selling classic cars company which sells vintage cars  (FEB 2023)

The key objective of this project is to understand the buying behavior of different countries based on the credit limit, quantity ordered by country, product vendor and product line.

Meaningful insights were extracted by performing Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) using Power BI measures like total cost, profit,  .

The cleaned dataset was transferred to MySQL, various queries were executed by applying appropriate normalization techniques to get the desired result.
